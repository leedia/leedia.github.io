About Me

Hi, I'm Dianne! I'm the girl with an owl on her head. I'm also a junior at Harvard concentrating in Computer Science on the Mind, Brain, and Behavior track. I'm particularly interested in the intersection between CS and other fields, especially psychology, neurobiology, literature, journalism, and human behavior.

Because of this, I've studied and worked on many projects in data science, visualization, and computational science & engineering. I've worked a lot with interactive journalism and enjoy applying what I learn about coding and statistics to other subjects I enjoy, like Ancient Egypt.

In my free time, you can find me working on a personal project, playing soccer, or banging away at the piano.
